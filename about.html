About Bright Path

The story, mission, and values behind the coaching practice.

Meet Emma Rivera

Emma founded Bright Path Coaching after her own career transition — leaving a decade in corporate finance to pursue work that felt aligned with who she actually was. That leap taught her firsthand how disorienting change can be, and how much a steady, knowledgeable guide can matter.

Since then, she's worked with hundreds of professionals facing career pivots, leadership challenges, and the quiet question of "what's next?" Her approach blends practical strategy with genuine curiosity about each client's story, so the plans she helps build are ones people can actually sustain.

Today, Emma leads Bright Path's individual coaching, group programs, and corporate workshops, all built around the same idea: transitions go better with someone in your corner.
